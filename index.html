About Me -

Transitioning from VR filmmaking to a budding Junior Software Developer, my journey reflects a blend of creative and technical prowess. My recent completion of the self-funded JUST IT training course epitomizes my dedication to diving into the IT realm, backed by a foundational grasp of HTML, JavaScript, and Python. The experiences I garnered from managing digital content and directing high-impact projects have fine-tuned my communication and problem-solving abilities, assets I aim to leverage in my next role as a Junior Developer. Driven by the same zeal that led to award-winning documentaries, I am eager to embrace challenges in a stimulating environment to further my IT skills. My goal is to develop as an IT professional, engaging in meaningful projects that push my technical boundaries and contribute to a larger positive impact. Through this transition, I aspire to meld my storytelling background with newfound software expertise, contributing a unique perspective to the software development domain.
